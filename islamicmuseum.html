About the Biennale

The Islamic Arts Biennale is a groundbreaking exhibition that redefines Islamic art for the 21st century. Set in the iconic Western Hajj Terminal, the Biennale showcases contemporary works alongside historical masterpieces, creating a dialogue between past and present.

This year's edition, titled "Awwal Bait" (First House), explores themes of spiritual journey, cultural exchange, and the universal values of Islam through immersive installations, calligraphy, textiles, and digital art.
