About the Festival

The Red Sea International Film Festival is a cinematic celebration that brings together storytellers from the Arab world and beyond. Held in the vibrant Al-Balad district, the festival features screenings, masterclasses, red carpet events, and awards ceremonies.

Now in its 5th edition, the festival continues to support emerging filmmakers and provide a platform for bold, innovative, and diverse voices in cinema.
